About me

This is who I was always born to be.

On a recent trip to Seattle, I was bombarded with questions from my family about what I was doing for work now. When they heard I was in Pest Control they almost didn't believe me until my parents confirmed the truth. I got responses like: "We thought you would have been building computers by now." or "programming was where I saw you in the future." No matter what they said all I could think was that they were right. When I was younger I was always on the computer. Playing games, having fun with my friends on social media, or building/fixing computers, routers, and networks for my family and my friends.

Something changed within me that cold day in Seattle. I finally realized what I was supposed to be doing and envisioned a path by wich to get there. Since that day I have been a sponge, soaking up all the information I could find on computer languages, libraries, and other tools used in the developer sphere. I have dedicated myself to learning the field and applying that knowledge here on this website and in the future projects I know on which I'll be working.
